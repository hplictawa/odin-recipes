Meaty Lasagna

Description:

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients:

Meaty Red Sauce:

  • 4 tbsp. Canola Oil
  • 1 med. White Onion
  • 3 pcs. Garlic Cloves
  • 750 g Ground Lean Beef
  • 1 can Jolly Mushroom Slices
  • 1 med. Red Bell Pepper
  • 2 tsp. Fresh Oregano/Basil or Dried Herbs (optional)
  • 900 g Spaghetti Sauce
  • 2 tsp. Salt & Pepper (adjust according to taste)
  • ½ pc. Beef Cubes (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp. White Sugar (to taste)

Bechamel Sauce (White Sauce):

  • 5 tbsp. Butter
  • 4 tbsp. All-Purpose Flour
  • 4 cups Fresh Milk
  • 2 tsp Iodized Salt
  • ½ tsp White Pepper

Pasta Noodles:

  • 500 g Lasagna Noodles (cook according to package instructions)
  • 1½ bar Quick Melt Cheese
  • Fresh Parsley or Basil (for toppings)

Steps:

Pasta Noodles:

  1. Boil 4 liters of Water & add 1 tbsp. Salt
  2. Put 500g Lasagna Noodles into a boiling water.
  3. Stir occasionally to prevent pasta from sticking together.
  4. Cook for 6 minutes or until al dente.
  5. Drain immediately, rinse, cool & set aside.

Meaty Red Sauce:

  1. Heat 4 tbsp. Oil in a pan.
  2. Saute 1 med. Onion and Garlic until soften.
  3. Add 750g Ground Beef and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Season with 1 tsp. Salt and ½ tsp. Pepper
  5. Add 1 cup Mushroom & ¼ cup Bell Pepper
  6. Season with a pinch of Salt & Pepper.
  7. Saute until veggies are softened.
  8. Add 2 tsp. fresh Oregano or dried Oregano & Basil (optional)
  9. Add 900g Spaghetti Sauce
  10. Simmer for 7 minutes on low heat.
  11. Add ½ Beef Cube, pinch of Pepper & 1 tbsp. Sugar
  12. Taste test and adjust according to your taste.

Bechamel Sauce (White Sauce):

  1. Add 5 tbsp. Butter in a saucepan & melt on low heat.
  2. Add 4 tbsp. All-Purpose Flour
  3. Stir until smooth & turns into light gold
  4. Add 1 cup of Fresh Milk at a time & whisk continuously until smooth.
  5. Bring mixture to a boil for 10 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and white pepper.
  7. Simmer until sauce thickens & set aside to cool.

Assembly:

  1. Put red sauce at the bottom of your baking dish & arrange Lasagna noodles on top.
  2. Spread white (Bechamel) sauce on top & arrange another layer 3-4 pcs. of Lasagna Noodles.
  3. Repeat until you have made a 3 layers of Red Sauce, Lasagna Noodles & White Sauce.
  4. Grate on top 1 bar Quick Melt Cheese
  5. Sprinkle some fresh or dried Parsley on top.
  6. Grease foil with oil and cover the Lasagna.
  7. Bake Lasagna in a pre-heated oven 180C-200C for 30 minutes.
  8. Let it cool for at least 10 mins. before serving.
  9. Serve and enjoy.
